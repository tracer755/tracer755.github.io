Welcome!



Troop 456 has been active for more than 50 years, and has proudly graduated over 90 eagle scouts. We are a boy-led troop of about 25 Scouts, composed largely of boys that attend Powell, Euclid, and Newton middle schools; and Arapahoe, Heritage, and Littleton high schools.
We welcome any new scouts!
We are part of the Black Beather District of the BSA
